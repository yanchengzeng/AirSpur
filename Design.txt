Genre: Top Down Scrolling shooter

Name: AirSpur

Description: Players can control a spur-like aircraft and are able to shoot different bullets
to kill enemies depending on their points. There are two levels in the game. For the first the 
level, as long as the player is able to drive the aircraft past the target distance, the player 
can advance to the next level. In level 2, the player will need to beat an ancient warrior 
who is capable of spinning and shooting powerful bullets.

Mechanics: The player can control the aircraft through arrow keys and shoot bullets with space key
to kill various enemies. There are speed-up and other options during the voyage. 

Modes: The aircraft will be upgraded in the more advanced level or in the first level if the player score high 
enough before the first level is over.
